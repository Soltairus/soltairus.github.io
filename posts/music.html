A Dash From Home (2022)

A Dash From Home was created for the OST Composing Jam #4 hosted by Lone Rabbit. Participants were tasked with making a soundtrack for a hypothetical game in 10 days. A Dash From Home is my submission, and you can read all about it’s development here.

Meri's Cafe (2022)

Meri's Cafe is a game developed by Majimjam which takes place in the universe for the game Hyades. It’s a visual novel where you play as Meriwether, mixing and serving coffee, tea and slushies for your customers. There is an extensive soundtrack made by a variety of people, but I composed tracks 10, Time To Cool Off, and 14, Shot In The Dark.
Meri's Cafe is available now for free now at itch.io and the soundtrack is available on Bandcamp, Spotify, Deezer and YouTube.
